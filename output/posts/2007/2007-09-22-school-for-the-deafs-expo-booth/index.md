Set-up and design for the Deaf students showcase booth at the _Panibagong Paraan_ Expo for Corporate Social Responsibility at the Megatrade Hall, Pasig City. The school  won a million pesos of project funding and the People's Choice award. [![164163619_c6c0dd05c1_o](images/164163619_c6c0dd05c1_o-300x243.jpg)](http://www.codeswitchstudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/164163619_c6c0dd05c1_o.jpg) [![164163620_ac092d1577_o](images/164163620_ac092d1577_o-300x247.jpg)](http://www.codeswitchstudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/164163620_ac092d1577_o.jpg)

From my previous incarnation as Media Specialist at the _DLS_\-_CSB_ School of Deaf Education and Applied Studies (_SDEAS_).  The kids I trained in Photoshop did the pictorial. Earnest and I did the promotional material. Visitors got to try out sign language and were rewarded with free brewed coffee, homemade chocolates, and learned some more deaf culture trivia.
