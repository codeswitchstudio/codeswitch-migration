(Prototype stage)

PhD Hive is an online platform that enables ​researchers to find reviewers specialised in their field, and obtain a detailed review in under 72 hours. It is an open and growing community where anyone can put themselves forward as a reviewer. However, ultimately it is user ratings that will determine how successful a reviewer is.
